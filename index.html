Email Management – Achieving Inbox Zero

A clean and organized email inbox, demonstrating effective email management practices. By keeping only essential messages visible and clearing out unnecessary clutter, the inbox stays easy to navigate and stress-free. Features like labels, categories, and filters help group emails by type.
